Audio samples for the paper: NON-PARALLEL SEQUENCE TO SEQUENCE VOICE CONVERSION FOR ARBITRARY SPEAKERS

  • The models are trained using our Mandarin corpus. Audios are resampled to 16kHz.
  • In the sequel, "seq2seq ctc_090 is the abbreviation of sequence-to-sequence voice conversion model with the threshold of 0.90 removing blank tokens. seq2seq ctc_099 is the same way. seq2seq ctc_onehot is the abbreviation of sequence-to-sequence voice conversion model with one-hot encoding, and all blank tokens are thrown away. F2F-VC is the abbreviation of frame-to-frame voice conversion model.(please refer to the paper for the details)"
  • some of utterances are clipped with the same content from source or converted ones in order to speed up page load.
